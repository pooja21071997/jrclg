Level of School : Junior College (XI & XII SCIENCE/ COMMERCE)
Medium: English

Science XI & XII

A) Compulsory Subject –

1) English
2) Physics
3) Chemistry
4) Environment Education
5) Health & Physical Education.

1) Marathi/IT

1) Biology
2) Mathematics

1) English
2) Economics
3) Book-Keeping & Accountancy

5) Environment Education
6) Health & Physical Education.

B) Any ONE Subject as a optional –
1) Marathi/IT

C) Any two instead of any one –
1) Secretarial Practice
2) Mathematics (Commerce)
