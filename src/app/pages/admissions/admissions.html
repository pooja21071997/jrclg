Admission Process

  • Admission to Std. XI (F.Y.J.C.) is through centralised admission process conducted by the Higher Secondary Board, Pune which will start after the declaration of Std. X results in Maharashtra. Students having passed Std. X (SSC / CBSE / ICSE / NOS or other equivalent examinations) are eligible for admission to F.Y.J.C. as per the merit and availability of seats.
  • Students who have passed from Boards other than Maharashtra State Board should get validation certification from Maharashtra State Board for participating in the admission process.
  • Students can seek admission to Std. XI directly as per the availability of seats. Direct applications to Std. XI may be considered for vacant seats after centralised admission process.
  • The admission will be finalised only after compliance of all the relevant certificates and documents.
  • Fees can be paid in two instalments. The first instalment should be paid at the time of admission and the second instalment in the first week of December.
  • Reservation for Backward category candidates will be as per the norms of Govt. of Maharashtra.
  • Students seeking admission to the Junior college may obtain the prospectus along with the application form from the college office by making a requisite payment.
  • Application form may be downloaded from the college website and the duly filled form may be sent to the college office along with the Demand Draft.

Documents Required

  • 3 Passport size photos
  • Statement of marks of SSC or other examination considered equivalent by the Board having passed in minimum five subjects with English as one of the subjects.
  • Original School / College Leaving Certificate / Transfer Certificate (duly countersigned by the examination officer, if applicable).
  • Passing Certificate of SSC or equivalent examination.
  • 3 Aadhar Card Xerox copy
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable).
  • Caste Certificate if any
  • Domicile Certificate.
