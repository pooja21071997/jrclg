Admission Process

  • Admission to Std. XI (F.Y.J.C.) is through the centralised admission process conducted by the Higher Secondary Board, Pune after declaration of Std. X results.
  • Students passing SSC / CBSE / ICSE / NOS or equivalent exams are eligible as per merit and seat availability.
  • Students from other Boards must obtain validation certification from Maharashtra State Board.
  • Direct admission to Std. XI may be considered for vacant seats after the centralised process.
  • Admission will be finalised only after submission of all required documents.

  • Reservation for backward category candidates will be as per Government of Maharashtra norms.
  • Application forms can be obtained from the college office or downloaded from the website.

Documents Required

  • 3 Passport Size Photographs
  • SSC Marksheet (Minimum 5 subjects including English)
  • Original Leaving / Transfer Certificate
  • SSC Passing Certificate
  • 3 Xerox Copies of Aadhar Card
  • Migration Certificate (If Applicable)
  • Caste Certificate (If Applicable)
  • Domicile Certificate
