ABOUT US

Shaping Future Leaders with Strong Values

Vivekanand Pratishthan established its Junior College in 2009, offering 11th and 12th Science & Commerce streams along with IT as a vocational subject. Affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board, the college is attached to the High School and is known for its academic excellence and discipline.

With well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer & IT laboratories and excellent sports facilities, the institution has produced hundreds of meritorious students who are well placed across the globe.
