About Us

Vivekanand Pratishthan has been established Junior college along with schooling education for facilating the students in 2009. College has 11th and 12th Science& CommerceCourses as well as IT as a Vocational subject.The college is attached to High school & it’s affiliated to State Board of Maharashtra. It provides education in Science & Commerce streams. The college has well equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computers,

& IT Laboratories and has good sports facilities. It is considered as one of the best Jr. Colleges of Jalgaon region due to its High academic standards and discipline. The college has churned out 100’s of meritorious students and they are well placed all over the world.
