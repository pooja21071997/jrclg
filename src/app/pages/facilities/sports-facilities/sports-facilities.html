Sports & Activities

Vivekanand Pratishthan Junior College encourages students to actively participate in various sports activities such as Cricket, Kabaddi, Football, Running, Basketball, and indoor games like Carom, Chess, and Badminton.

The college provides separate grounds for outdoor games and organizes yoga camps for boys and girls. Sports play a vital role in building leadership qualities, discipline, confidence, and team spirit among students.

Annual Sports Week is organized in the second term of every academic year, promoting inclusive development. Our students proudly represent the university at various competitions, with several achieving state and national-level recognition.
