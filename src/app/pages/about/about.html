ABOUT INSTITUTION

Vivekanand Pratishthan Junior College, Jalgaon

Vivekanand Pratishthan is a well-known educational organization in the Khandesh region for over 25 years, nurturing not only academic excellence but also essential life values. The institution has consistently worked towards building an educated and culturally enriched society.

Established in 2009, the Junior College offers 11th and 12th Science & Commerce streams along with IT as a vocational subject. Affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board, the college is attached to the High School and is recognized for its discipline and high academic standards.

With well-equipped laboratories in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computers & IT and strong sports facilities, the college has produced hundreds of meritorious students who are successfully placed worldwide.

Education is meant to make life better, help personal growth, enhance thinking abilities, and lead to good morals.

— Dr. Avinash Ramachandra Acharya
