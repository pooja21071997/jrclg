Vivekanand Pratishthan Junior College Jalgaon

Vivekanand Pratishthan is the well-known educational organization in the region of khandesh since 25 yearswhere not only education but essential values of life are also taught to the students. Vivekananda Pratishthan has been maintaining this identity for the last three decades. Continuing work is underway to create education in the grassroots of the society through the establishment of educated society. Various educational institutes are started under the one roof of vivekanand Pratishthan.

Vivekanand Pratishthan has been established Junior college along with schooling education for facilating the students in 2009. College has 11th and 12th Science& CommerceCourses as well as IT as a Vocational subject.The college is attached to High school & it’s affiliated to State Board of Maharashtra. It provides education in Science & Commerce streams. The college has well equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computers, & IT Laboratories and has good sports facilities. It is considered as one of the best Jr. Colleges of Jalgaon region due to its High academic standards and discipline. The college has churned out 100’s of meritorious students and they are well placed all over the world.

Education is meant to make life better, help personal growth, enhance thinking abilities, and lead to good morals.
